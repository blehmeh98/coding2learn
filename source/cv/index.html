Personal Profile

I'm a passionate teacher with over 12 years experience in the disciplines of Science, Computing and Martial Arts. I've worked in a variety of schools; as a teacher, a curriculum leader and a network manager, and have consistently been rated Outstanding by OfSTED and my line managers.

I'm as comfortable in the Terminal as I am in the Classroom, and believe that both software and education should be free (as in speech and as in beer).

I'm an active blogger, and ramble at www.coding2learn.org. I've had the pleasure of hitting the front page of Hacker News twice, and had one of my posts go viral on Reddit.
